Imagine Dragons

My favourite band

Imagine Dragons is an American music band founded in Las Vegas, Nevada, in 2008.
The band performs popular genres such as pop rock, indie rock and electro pop.
The band gained popularity thanks to their hits such as "Radioactive", "Demons", "Believer" and others.
The band is known for its energetic and emotional music, as well as for its use in various musical styles.
