Imagine Dragons

Information

Imagine Dragons - is an American music group that gained immense popularity for their energetic and emotional music.
The band was founded in Las Vegas, Nevada in 2008.
The band consists of Dan Reynolds (vocals, guitar), Wayan Sermont (bass, backing vocals), Daniel Plaitman (drums), Wayan Dean (guitar) and Daniel Wayne (keyboards).

Popular songs

  • "Demons"
  • "Believer"
  • "Thunder"
  • "Whatever It Takes"
  • "It's Time"

Social activity

Imagine Dragons actively participates in charity events and social initiatives.
The band supports various philanthropic organizations and is intended for charitable purposes.
